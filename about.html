About Us

Our Story

At Paws & Tails, we believe every puppy deserves a loving home. Founded in 2023, our mission is to connect puppy lovers with their perfect furry friends. We strive to ensure that each puppy we place in a home is healthy, well-socialized, and ready to become a cherished member of your family.

Our Vision

To create a world where every puppy is happy, healthy, and loved. We are committed to promoting responsible pet ownership and supporting our community through education and outreach programs.
