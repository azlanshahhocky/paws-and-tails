AAFCO Nutritional Guidelines for Puppies

Essential Nutrients

  • Protein: 22-32% (depending on breed)
  • Fat: 8-20%
  • Calcium: 1.0-1.8%

Feeding Schedule

  • 8-12 weeks: 4 meals daily
  • 3-6 months: 3 meals daily
  • 6+ months: 2 meals daily
