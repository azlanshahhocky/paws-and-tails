Tips for Buying a Puppy

What to Look For

  • Health Records: Always request vaccination records and health certificates
  • Meet the Parents: If possible, meet the puppy's parents to assess temperament and health
  • Living Conditions: Observe where the puppies are kept - it should be clean and safe
  • Behavior: A healthy puppy should be active, curious, and friendly
  • Physical Appearance: Check for clear eyes, clean ears, healthy coat, and no signs of illness

Questions to Ask Sellers

  • What is the puppy's age and breed?
  • Has the puppy been vaccinated and dewormed?
  • What is the puppy's temperament and personality?
  • Are there any known health issues or genetic conditions?
  • What is the return or health guarantee policy?
  • Can I meet the puppy's parents?
  • What type of food is the puppy currently eating?

Red Flags to Watch Out For

  • Seller refuses to let you visit the puppies
  • No health records or vaccination documentation
  • Puppies appear lethargic, sick, or malnourished
  • Poor living conditions or overcrowding
  • Seller pressures you to buy immediately
  • Price seems too good to be true
  • Seller has many different breeds available at once

Tips for Selling Your Puppy

Preparing Your Listing

  • Take clear, well-lit photos from multiple angles
  • Record short videos showing the puppy's personality
  • Be honest about the puppy's health and temperament
  • Include all relevant information (breed, age, vaccinations)
  • Set a fair price based on breed, pedigree, and market rates

Responsible Selling Practices

  • Only sell puppies that are at least 8 weeks old
  • Provide complete health and vaccination records
  • Screen potential buyers to ensure a good home
  • Be available to answer questions
  • Consider offering a health guarantee
  • Provide guidance on care and feeding

Legal Requirements

  • Check local laws regarding puppy sales
  • Ensure all vaccinations are up to date
  • Provide accurate breed information
  • Disclose any known health issues
  • Keep records of the sale
