Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly will I receive a response?

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business hours. Urgent matters are prioritized.

Can I visit your facility?

Yes! We welcome visits by appointment. Please call or email us to schedule a time.

Do you offer emergency support?

For emergency pet health concerns, please contact your local veterinarian or emergency animal hospital immediately.

What information should I include in my message?

Please provide your name, contact information, and a detailed description of your inquiry. For adoption requests, include information about your living situation and experience with pets.
