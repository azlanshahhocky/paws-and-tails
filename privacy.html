Privacy Policy

Data Protection at Paws & Tails

1. Data Collection

We collect the following personal data:

  • Contact information (name, email)
  • Order history
  • Usage data (website interactions)

Cookies

We use cookies to improve user experience and gather analytics. You can manage cookies in your browser settings.

2. Data Usage

  • Contacting you about puppy orders
  • Providing customer support
  • Customizing website content and recommendations
  • Internal analysis and improvements

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or lease your data to third parties. We may share data with:

  • Logistics partners for puppy deliveries
  • Payment processors for transaction handling
  • Legal authorities when required by law

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including encryption and secure servers. However, no data transmission is 100% secure.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and rectify your personal data
  • Delete your data
  • Restrict processing of your data
  • Object to data processing
  • Data portability

Contact us at privacy@pawsanadtails.site to exercise these rights.

6. Third-Party Services

We use third-party services, including:

  • Google Analytics for website analytics
  • Stripe/PayPal for payment processing
  • Shipping companies for puppy deliveries

Please review these providers' privacy policies.

7. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect data from children under 16. If you are a parent and believe your child has provided data, contact us immediately.
